Delete Employee Record

by: Benjamin Liles

Note: Due to browser limitations, this app cannot delete files from your system automatically.

To delete an employee file:

  1. Open your employees folder.
  2. Locate the employee file (e.g., bliles.txt).
  3. Right-click and choose Delete (or press Del).

This step ensures that employee data is removed from your system manually and securely.
